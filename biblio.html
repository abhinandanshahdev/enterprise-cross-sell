Workplace Dysfunction Analysis & Solutions

Executive Summary

17 critical organizational issues identified across three domains: Attitude (4), Behavior (7), and Culture (6). Root causes center on low psychological safety, unclear accountability, and over-rigid processes that stifle initiative and ownership.

üöÄ Implementation Priority Matrix

QUICK WINS (0-3 months)

  1. Clear decision rights - Delegation matrix
  2. Regular feedback cycles - Weekly 1:1s
  3. Visual management - Problem tracking boards
  4. "Try first" policies - Reduce escalation

MEDIUM TERM (3-6 months)

  1. Cross-functional KPIs - Shared accountability
  2. Knowledge sharing incentives - Documentation rewards
  3. Approval threshold policies - Streamline decisions
  4. Innovation time allocation - 20% improvement time

LONG TERM (6-12 months)

  1. Psychological safety training - Cultural transformation
  2. Management coaching programs - Leadership development
  3. Bottom-up innovation systems - Idea tournaments
  4. Balanced measurement systems - Beyond gaming metrics

Bottom Line: These issues are interconnected. Address psychological safety and clear accountability first, then build systematic improvements on that foundation.
